This website is aimed at researchers and small companies in order to find and share prepreg (preimpregnated) fabrics. This will prevent the waste of expensive materials by sharing them before they go out of life. Futhermore, it will enable you to get hold of small quantities of material which is becoming difficult as the composites industry consolidates into large companies. Please register and begin searching for prepregs or entering your own materials to share with others.

If you have any problems using the site please get in touch
